Profile Of the Firm

Islam Jahid & Co. Chartered Accountants provides a broad range of financial and consultancy services. We are able to combine a detailed knowledge of local statute, custom regulations and requirements with the resources and skills of a highly motivated and dedicated team of professionals available with the firm. Islam Jahid & Co. Chartered Accountants is one of the audit and consultancy firm registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Bangladesh. We provide all sorts of professional services. Our object is for the benefit of our valued clients with the promise to do our best to maximize satisfaction and in order to provide consistent high quality services to the clients. Islam Jahid & Co. has multidisciplinary knowledge specially various consultancy such as investment decision, taxation, audit, internal audit, accounts preparation, business information system, information technology, statutory and liquidation services, valuation services, company law, VAT services, various types of payroll services including provident and other fund, management consultancy services encompassing, Management Training, Recruitment and Selection, Personnel Management, inter alia, Asset Valuation, valuation of business and share, Corporate Finance, Treasury Management, Baseline Survey, Project profile/project proposal, IT Outsourcing, Accountancy Outsourcing, Tax Compliance, Tax Planning, deigning of accounting, internal control operational systems for organization, secretarial services, company affairs (Company formation, share transfer, share allotment/registration, yearly return submission, company liquidation), cash incentive collection from the concerned authority, company registration & work permit from Board of Investment (BOI), Chamber permission from Chamber of Commerce and Industry (CCI), Import Registration Certificate (IRC), Export Registration Certificate (ERC), from Chief Controller of Import and Export, Visa extension of foreign national of Department of Immigration and passports and visa, Trade License of City Corporation Office/ Union Council, Security Clearance of Foreign national from Ministry of Homes, companyâ€™s brand registration from Ministry of Industries, liaison, head office/ branch office permission from the concerned department, and other advisory services.

Profile Of Partners

MD. SHAMIM AHSAN qualified as a Chartered Accountant in 2008 from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Bangladesh (ICAB), within his article ship from December 1994 to May 1998 with a reputed firm named Rahman Thakur & Co., he gained wide and varied experience in the fields of auditing, accounting and taxation. He has a vast experience in some of renowned Group of Industry in Bangladesh like as PRAN GROUP, Orion Group, Padma Group, DIRD GROUP from September 1999 to June 2013. He is practicing with Islam Jahid & Co. Chartered Accountants as a Partner since 1st July 2013. He has overall knowledge of Accounts / Finance / Audit / Tax / VAT /Company affairs as well as administrative management preferably in a multinational or international organization or reputed company or a group of companies environment. MD. SHAMIM AHSAN now is a Fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Bangladesh (ICAB) and his ICAB Enrollment number is 1047.
